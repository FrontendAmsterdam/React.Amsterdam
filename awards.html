React Open Source Awards

We’re looking specifically at open source projects or initiatives, with or without a code, but following an open and transparent culture, open for collaboration and contributions from any person interested.

For some cases, projects that are not sharing source code publicly, but are without a doubt considered non-profit, non-commercial, could be also participating in the awards

Breakthrough of the year

Project contributing the most to the React ecosystem, adding new dimensions to it and possibilities for further development. New concepts/ideas with big future potential and good first realization in 2017.
